OVERVIEW

A wild horse, descended from Spanish colonial horses, known for their strength, speed, and spirit. Mustangs roam the American West, symbolizing freedom and the untamed spirit of the frontier.

DESCRIPTION

Country of Origin   North America
Weight   800 to 1,000 lb (360 to 450 kg)
Height   4 to 15.2 hands high (56 to 62 inches at the shoulder).
Color   Bay, black, chestnut, or gray. Occasional dominant white, sabino, or rabicano patterns.

CHARACTERISTICS

HEAD

Mustangs typically have a broad, slightly convex or straight profile. Their heads are proportionate to their bodies.


BUILD

Mustangs are known for their muscular and athletic build, with a strong, compact body that allows for endurance and agility.
They have long, sturdy legs with hard hooves, making them well-suited for rough terrain and long-distance travel.


TEMPERAMENT

Mustangs are known for their spirited and independent nature. They are intelligent and curious, often exhibiting a strong flight response due to their wild ancestry.


OVERALL

ustangs exude a sense of strength, freedom, and grace. Their wild heritage is evident in their alert demeanor and powerful stance, making them iconic symbols of the American West.
