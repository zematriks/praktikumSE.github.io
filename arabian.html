OVERVIEW

The Arabian horse is one of the oldest and most revered horse breeds in the world, known for its striking beauty, intelligence, stamina, and friendly disposition.

DESCRIPTION

Country of Origin   Middle East
Weight   800 to 1,000 lb (360 to 450 kg)
Height   14.1 to 15.1 hands (57 to 61 inches, 145 to 155 cm)
Color   Bay, black, chestnut, or gray. Occasional dominant white, sabino, or rabicano patterns.

CHARACTERISTICS

HEAD

The head is small and finely chiseled, with a dished or concave profile, meaning there is a slight inward curve from the forehead to the muzzle. .


BUILD

Arabians have a small, refined frame with a short back, strong legs, and a wide chest. This build is ideal for endurance and agility.The Arabian's head is often described as "dishy,"
meaning it has a concave profile from the forehead to the muzzle.


TEMPERAMENT

The Persano horse is known for its gentle and indomitable temperament, making it versatile for various riding disciplines.


OVERALL

The Persano is an all-rounder, suitable for riding, dressage, and other equestrian disciplines. Its rugged yet elegant build makes it adaptable to different riding styles
